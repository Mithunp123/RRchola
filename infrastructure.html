World-Class Infrastructure

Advanced facilities designed to deliver exceptional patient care in a sanctuary of healing and precision.

Robotics Surgery

RR Chola Hospital offers advanced robotic-assisted surgery for complex procedures that require exceptional precision. Our operating teams use modern navigation systems and minimally invasive techniques that reduce blood loss, support faster healing, and improve clinical outcomes for patients.

ICU

Our intensive care units are designed for high-acuity treatment with round-the-clock observation, expert intensivists, and advanced life-support systems. Every ICU bay is planned to provide safety, efficiency, and a calm environment for both patients and families.

Birth Suites

RR Chola Hospital's birth suites combine medical readiness with a warm and private setting. From labor support to neonatal monitoring, each suite is equipped to provide a safe and comfortable childbirth experience under the care of skilled obstetric and pediatric teams.

Dialysis

Our dialysis unit maintains strict infection-control standards and dependable treatment quality for renal care patients. The unit is staffed by trained nephrology professionals and supported with advanced monitoring systems to ensure comfort and clinical stability during each session.

World-class Laboratory

The hospital's laboratory services are powered by automated analyzers and quality-controlled workflows for fast and accurate diagnostics. From routine pathology to specialized tests, our lab supports every department with timely reports that enable better treatment decisions.

Newborn ICU

Our neonatal intensive care unit is built for high-risk newborn care with incubators, respiratory support, and continuous monitoring. A specialized neonatology team provides immediate intervention and compassionate support for families during the most sensitive stages of treatment.

Emergency Response

The emergency department operates 24/7 with rapid triage, critical care access, and coordinated trauma protocols. Our teams are trained to respond quickly and efficiently to stabilize patients during medical emergencies, accidents, and urgent interventions.

Ambulance Services

Our ambulance fleet is equipped with cardiac monitors, ventilators, emergency medications, and trained paramedic staff. These mobile critical-care units ensure safe patient transfer and rapid pre-hospital care across a wide range of emergency situations.

Endoscopic Suite

RR Chola Hospital's endoscopy suites support advanced upper and lower GI procedures with precision imaging and specialized intervention tools. Our gastroenterology team performs both diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in a safe and controlled environment.

Restaurant & Cafeteria

The hospital campus includes a clean and welcoming restaurant-cafeteria for patients, attendants, and visitors. The menu is planned to offer nutritious choices and comfortable dining spaces that support rest, recovery, and daily convenience.
